Learning about the number of people in your facility who have tested positive for COVID-19 or have symptoms will help mobilize the public health response and support that your facility may need.

COVID-19 symptoms include:

  • New or worsening cough
  • Fever (temperature over 100.4 F)
  • Trouble breathing/shortness of breath
  • Body aches
  • Tiredness/fatigue
